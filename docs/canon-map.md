# Canon Map — Stunspot’s Guide to Macroeconomics

This canon is organized as a sequence of **13 source reports**, labeled **A** through **M**. The sequence moves from monetary ontology to liquidity mechanics, macro-causal architecture, policy and institutional systems, market regimes, information dynamics, crisis mechanics, diagnostics, and strategic action.

The source reports are the canonical individual units. Compiled packs and the omnibus are convenience formats for AI/RAG ingestion.

## Directory Policy

- `docs/` is the navigation and guidance layer.
- `knowledge-packs/by-report/` contains the individual source-report corpus.
- `knowledge-packs/compiled-packs/` contains grouped upload packs.
- `knowledge-packs/omnibus/` contains the whole-corpus bundle.
- There is no `docs/reports/` directory.

## Sequence Logic

The canon has a deliberate build order:

1. **A-C establish the foundations**: what money is, how liquidity moves, and how macro regimes form.
2. **D-F map institutional transmission**: central banks, fiscal states, sovereign debt, banks, credit, and intermediation.
3. **G-I connect liquidity to markets and capital formation**: asset pricing, global trade/currency hierarchy, venture finance, and speculative expansion.
4. **J-M move from perception to action**: narrative liquidity, crisis cascades, diagnostic systems, and institutional strategy.
