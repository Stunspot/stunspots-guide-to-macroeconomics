## What This Canon Is

*Stunspot’s Guide to Macroeconomics* is a structured macro-financial doctrine corpus designed primarily for AI/RAG ingestion. It gives models a stable conceptual map for reasoning about money, credit, collateral, liquidity, policy transmission, asset regimes, sovereign power, information markets, crisis cascades, diagnostic signals, and institutional strategy.

The canon is human-readable, but its deeper purpose is operational: to improve the quality of model-assisted macroeconomic analysis by giving the model source-traceable terminology, report boundaries, causal frameworks, and reusable diagnostic structures.

## Recommended Loading Path

For most AI/RAG workflows, use the four compiled packs in [`knowledge-packs/compiled-packs/`](https://github.com/Stunspot/stunspots-guide-to-macroeconomics/tree/main/knowledge-packs/compiled-packs). They preserve the canon’s volume structure while avoiding the friction of uploading 13 separate reports or one very large omnibus file.

Use source reports when you need fine-grained retrieval or citation. Use the omnibus for archive, local search, or strong long-context systems.
